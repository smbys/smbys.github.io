Come on Screen

Visit Project Page

Come on Screen is a presentation support tool that allows the presenters to present with gestures by adding the presenter's capture video on the presentation slide. I originated the basic idea, and my friends and I are working together to develop it.

[IMPORTANT!] We plan the major update of Come on Screen by 2021Q2. Don't miss it!

DATE: 2020.04.10 - Present

WORKS AT: Dendenlab. (Maker)

STATUS: Preparing the major update
